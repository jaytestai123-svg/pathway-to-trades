Available Funding

Grants that pay for
your training

These are real federal and state programs — not loans. You don't pay them back.

🏛️

WIOA Individual Training Account

Up to $15,000

The main federal workforce grant. Covers tuition, books, tools, and certifications at approved schools.

📚

Federal Pell Grant

Up to $7,395/yr

Need-based federal grant for students. Can be stacked with WIOA for maximum funding.

🏭

Trade Adjustment Assistance

Full tuition + stipend

For workers displaced by foreign trade or outsourcing. Includes a monthly living stipend while in school.

🏔️

Colorado State Grant

Up to $5,000

State-funded supplement available through Colorado Workforce Centers. Fills gaps left by federal grants.
